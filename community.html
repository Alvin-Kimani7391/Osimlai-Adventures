Wildlife
Conservation Success

Saving the Elephants of Amboseli

Through anti-poaching patrols and community education, we've helped reduce elephant poaching in the Amboseli ecosystem by 67% over the past three years. Local rangers, trained and funded by Osimlai, now protect over 1,200 elephants across the region.

"The elephants are our heritage. With Osimlai's support, we're ensuring they survive for generations to come."
Amboseli, Kenya Since 2022 45 rangers trained
Education

Building Classrooms in Rural Tanzania

In partnership with local communities, we've constructed 12 new classrooms across 8 villages, providing education to over 800 children who previously had no access to schooling. Our programs also provide books, uniforms, and teacher training.

"Before this school, our children walked 10 kilometers to the nearest classroom. Now, they can learn right here, in their own village."
Arusha, Tanzania Since 2021 800+ students

Healthcare Access

Mobile Clinics for Remote Communities

Our mobile health clinics reach over 15 remote villages each month, providing essential healthcare services to communities that would otherwise go without. From maternal health to malaria prevention, we're saving lives across rural Uganda.

"The mobile clinic saved my daughter's life. She had malaria and we had no way to get to the hospital. Now, we have hope."
Western Uganda Since 2023 5,000+ patients served
