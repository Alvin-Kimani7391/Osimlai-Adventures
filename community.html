Real Stories

Impact Stories from the Field

Every project tells a story of hope, resilience, and community spirit — from coastal towns to highland villages. Here are just a few.

Wildlife
Conservation Success

Saving the Elephants of Amboseli

Through anti-poaching patrols and community education, we've helped reduce elephant poaching in the Amboseli ecosystem by 67% over the past three years. Local rangers, trained and funded by International Volunteer Home Tz, now protect over 1,200 elephants across the region.

"The elephants are our heritage. With this support, we're ensuring they survive for generations to come."
Amboseli, Kenya Since 2022 45 rangers trained
Education

Building Classrooms in Rural Tanzania

In partnership with local communities, we've constructed 12 new classrooms across 8 villages, providing education to over 800 children who previously had no access to schooling. Our programs also provide books, uniforms, and teacher training.

"Before this school, our children walked 10 kilometers to the nearest classroom. Now, they can learn right here, in their own village."
Arusha, Tanzania Since 2021 800+ students
Education
Healthcare
Healthcare Access

Mobile Clinics for Remote Communities

Our mobile health clinics reach over 15 remote villages each month, providing essential healthcare services to communities that would otherwise go without. From maternal health to malaria prevention, we're saving lives across rural Uganda.

"The mobile clinic saved my daughter's life. She had malaria and we had no way to get to the hospital. Now, we have hope."
Western Uganda Since 2023 5,000+ patients served
Environment

Reforesting the Rift Valley

Working with local communities, we've planted over 450 indigenous trees across 5 hectares of degraded land in the Rift Valley. This initiative not only restores habitat for wildlife but also combats soil erosion and supports local livelihoods.

"The trees are bringing life back to our land. We see more birds, more water, and more hope for our children."
Rift Valley, Kenya Since 2023 450+ trees planted

How We Give Back

Every booking contributes to one of our core community programs. Here's where your impact goes.

Wildlife Conservation

Funding anti-poaching patrols, wildlife research, and habitat protection across East Africa's most vulnerable ecosystems.

Education & Schools
