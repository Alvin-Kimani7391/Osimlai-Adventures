Rooted In East Africa

Live and volunteer alongside the many peoples of East Africa

Beyond the placement itself, every program is an invitation into the daily life of the region — from Swahili fishing towns on the coast, to Chagga farming villages on Kilimanjaro's slopes, Kikuyu and Luo neighborhoods, Kalenjin highlands, and Maasai and Waarusha communities near Arusha. Our local coordinators are drawn from these same communities, so your welcome is genuine, not staged for tourists.

  • Home-stays and cultural exchange with local families across the region
  • Swahili language basics for every volunteer, plus local phrases where relevant
  • Community-led projects, never top-down "voluntourism"
